Club History

Real Madrid was founded in 1902 in Madrid, Spain. It is one of the most successful football clubs in history, known for its impressive achievements in both domestic and international competitions.

Description of Competitions

UEFA Champions League: The UEFA Champions League is the most prestigious club competition in European football. Real Madrid holds the record for the most titles won (14), showcasing its dominance on the international stage.

La Liga: La Liga is Spain's top-tier football league. With 35 titles, Real Madrid is one of the most successful teams in Spanish football history.

Copa del Rey: The Copa del Rey is Spain's primary domestic cup competition. Real Madrid has won this trophy 20 times, proving its strength in knockout tournaments.

FIFA Club World Cup: This competition brings together the top club teams from different continents. Real Madrid has won this international title 5 times, highlighting its global success.
